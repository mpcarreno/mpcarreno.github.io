Electronics Engineer with expertise in signal processing, digital health systems, IoT, and secure networked technologies. My background combines a strong analytical foundation with practical experience in the design, evaluation, and protection of complex electronic and information systems.

I have over two years of professional experience in IT and cybersecurity environments, with solid knowledge of cloud services, digital forensics, and incident response. I am proficient in working with cloud infrastructures, firewalls, SIEM and EDR platforms, and possess a strong foundation in Windows and Linux systems, networking, and security monitoring.


Interests
------ 

My professional interests focus on advancing the integration of security principles into the design and operation of complex electronic and networked systems, with particular emphasis on IoT and digital health applications. I am interested in research that enhances system resilience, reliability, and trustworthiness, and explores how cybersecurity can support risk mitigation and sustainable technological development, especially in systems that process sensitive or large scale health and sensor data.
